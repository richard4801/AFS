Payment Details

Used by AFS to send your monthly payout. Choose what works best for you.

Best for African Writers

Sending directly to your Raenest tag keeps fees significantly lower on your end — you receive closer to your full payout. Create a free account at raenest.com if you haven't already.

Best for International Writers

Good option for writers outside Africa. Note that Payoneer charges a fee when you withdraw to your local bank — you won't receive the full amount. Need help setting up? Contact your editor.

Direct Bank Transfer (Last Resort)

Bank wire transfers carry the highest fees. Only use this if neither option above is available to you.
